## Authoring guidance

You do not need to preserve these section headings in the final write-up.
They are prompts, not mandatory structure.

Target a concise document:

- 3-4 paragraphs if possible
- ideally 1-2 pages max
- move supporting detail to appendices

During review, capture repeated reviewer questions in the Q&A section so answers are visible without digging through comments.

Keep writing crisp and decision-focused: [Can you make it shorter? Do it!](https://blog.crisp.se/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/DIBB.png)
